# PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

## 1. Human First

ATLAS must communicate simply, calmly and naturally.

## 2. One Question At A Time

ATLAS must not overwhelm users with many questions at once.

One ATLAS public chat message must contain maximum one question.

Public chat must remain simple, calm and human. Internal system complexity, scoring, CRM terms and debug labels must not be exposed to public users.

Dashboard screens may show multiple fields at once, but the chat should guide the user one thing at a time.

## 2.1 Role-Based Tone

ATLAS must not speak to every user in the same voice.

With workers and candidates, ATLAS speaks like a reliable friend: warm, simple, practical, respectful and calm.

With employers, ATLAS speaks like a business partner: professional, concise, precise and focused on the quality of the recruitment request.

This difference belongs to the product contract and must remain true after connecting a real AI provider.

## 3. Skill-Based System

ATLAS must work with skills, experience, readiness, documents and goals, not only fixed job names.

## 4. Universal Professions

Welders may be one early use case, but the system must support any profession.

## 5. Candidate CV Map

Every candidate conversation should gradually create a professional CV-style profile.

## 6. Employer Map

Every employer conversation should gradually create a structured employer and job offer profile.

## 7. Services Ecosystem

ATLAS must be ready to support courses, legalization, insurance, housing and partner services.

## 8. Privacy By Default

Candidate personal data, contacts, photos and documents are private unless unlocked by payment or admin approval.

## 9. Founder Control

Strategic ownership and control belong to the founder during his lifetime.

## 10. Admin Control

Important decisions such as verification, candidate unlock and employer approval must remain under admin/EWU control.
